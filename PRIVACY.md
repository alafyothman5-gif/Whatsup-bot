Privacy Policy

WhatsPrice Bot is a WhatsApp assistant used to answer customer questions about product prices and availability.

We receive WhatsApp messages sent by users to the bot only to understand the request and reply.

We do not sell user data.
We do not share user messages with advertisers.

Messages may be processed by Meta WhatsApp Business Platform and by our server to provide the service.

To request deletion of messages or data, contact the business owner.
